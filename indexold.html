Who is Leading Techs?

Leading Techs is a privately held Computer Networking/IT company founded in 2013. Our headquarters is located Las Vegas, NV. We are a smaller business with about 30-50 employees.

Leading Techs brings together years of combined experience in Networking, WiFi, Security, and Low Voltage Cabling to provide a complete network installation and upgrade solution.

We specialize in large infrastructure network design and implementation. Our company has designed and installed enterprise networks for hundreds of hotels and offices including Marriott, Diamond Resorts International, Hilton, and Westin Hotels. Leading Techs has also designed and installed multiple networks for social networking companies and Internet Service Providers (ISP's) including iBAHN, Guest-Tek, Blueprint RF, Single Digits, Facebook, Swisscom, Roomlinx, and AT&T.

 

Through support of multiple vendors and products, the best solutions can be combined that will fit budgets large and small.
